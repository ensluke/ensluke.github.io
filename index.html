Voltorb-flip is a game similar to Minesweeper and Sudoku, where the placement of the bombs are given for each row and column. The goal of the game is to uncover all of the 2 and 3 tiles on a given board and move up to higher levels which have higher point totals.

The numbers on the side and bottom of the game board denote the sum of the tiles and how many bombs are present in that row/column, respectively. Each tile you flip multiplies your collected points by that value. Once you uncover all of the 2 and 3 tiles, all of the points you gained this level will be added to your total and you'll go up one level to a max of 8. If you flip over a Voltorb, you lose all your points from the current level and risk going down to a lower level.
